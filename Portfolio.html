HTML5 is the fifth and latest major revision of the Hypertext Markup Language (HTML), which is the standard markup language used for structuring and presenting content on the World Wide Web. bootstrap

A "bootstrap" can refer to several things, primarily a strap on a boot, or the concept of self-starting or self-reliant development. In a computing context, it often refers to the initial process of loading a program or operating system into a computer. More broadly, "bootstrapping" signifies achieving something through one's own efforts and resources, often from a small beginning.
